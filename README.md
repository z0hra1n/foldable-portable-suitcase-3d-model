# foldable-portable-suitcase-3d-model
Portable foldable suitcase thingy for carrying bags and stuff while shopping or travelling.
it should be assembled as shown in the assembled model. There are three types of pegs for connecting everything. It is very tiring carrying bags around while shopping or travelling theis hase been made to make our lives easier.In the printable model, to scale it up it may not fit on small printers. i advice people with small printers print parts separately by splitting the different parts.
